02 — Problem

Demand is surging.
Supply isn’t keeping up.

People are searching for these experiences and not finding what they need. The current landscape has gaps.

Fragmented Offerings
Current models don’t incorporate multiple modalities under one roof. Consumers cobble together separate studios, spas, and apps.
Instructor Dependency
Traditional wellness businesses depend entirely on instructors for content. The experience is inconsistent and the business is fragile.
Social Disconnection
Unprecedented levels of anxiety and depression. People need physical spaces for genuine connection — not another app.
Low-End Experience
Most wellness spaces feel clinical or gym-like. There is no premium, immersive option for the growing audience that values experience quality.
